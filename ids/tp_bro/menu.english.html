Network Controller

Virtual Appliance

With the proliferation of user provisioned, prosumer devices (Smart Phones, iPhones, iPads, sensors, cameras…), enterprises need to evolve their access control and internal threat protection in order to ensure data security and compliance.

Mancala Networks has developed the Network Controller in order to provide enterprises and MSPs with the tools they need to deploy real-time, context aware visibility into who is doing what on their network as well as tight control over what is allowed.

Deployed easily along side existing infrastructure with no need for expensive upgrades or end point agents, the Network Controller starts adding value immediately in terms of network visibility and real-time control. Its consolidated management functionality enables a rapid, economical and high asset re-use deployment model for new security services including: a simple, staged migration to 802.1X centric identity aware access control, simple and secure guest access, controlled admission of new types of devices and internal threat prevention.

Trial version: 90 day trial license — Unlimited devices.
Limited to real-time discovery and inventory components.
Environment: VMware.
Minimum configuration: 1 virtual CPU — 1 GB of RAM — 30 GB of disk space.
